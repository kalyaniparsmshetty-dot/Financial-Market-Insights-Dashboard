# Financial Market Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides insights into the Indian stock market by analyzing companies across multiple industries. The dashboard helps identify market leaders, industry performance, company concentration, and stock price trends.

### 4. Average Current Price by Industry
Compares stock price levels across industries.

### 5. Top 10 Industries by Average ROCE
Analyzes capital efficiency across industries.

## Key Insights

- Finance dominates market capitalization.
- Market value is concentrated among top companies.
- Industry performance varies significantly.
- Stock prices differ widely across sectors.
- High-ROCE industries demonstrate stronger capital efficiency.

## Dataset

The dataset contains financial information of 4,668 companies across 107 industries, including:

- Company Name
- Industry
- Market Capitalization
- Current Price
- Sales
- Profit After Tax
- EPS
- ROCE
- Promoter Holding
